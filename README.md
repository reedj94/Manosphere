# Manosphere

**A darkly comedic, satirical open-world RPG about hustle culture, crypto scams, and toxic masculinity.**

## Premise

You've just been fired from your soul-crushing admin job at a faceless Northern English tech company. Scrolling through **Scammagram** (the in-game social media platform), the algorithm serves you a reel from **BS Twatty Titty** — an influencer promising you can be a trillionaire if you just "commit to the lifestyle."

What follows is a journey through four regions — **Consett, London, Miami, and Dubai** — as you climb the **Pyramid of Manospherical Success**, trading **Crapto** (parody cryptocurrency), **chasing clout**, and **injecting testosterone** while a mysterious woman named **Grotty Green** pulls the strings for her own revenge.

## Features

- **4 Open Zones** — Consett (grey, Northern, bleak) → London (neon, busy, fake) → Miami (bright, seductive, hollow) → Dubai (golden, excessive, lonely)
- **Crapto Trading Terminal** — Full trading interface with real-world crypto price feeds, technical indicators, and a **bot scripting system** where players can write automated strategies (EMA, RSI, MACD, custom indicators)
- **3 Core Mini-Games** — Crapto Trading, Clout Chasing (dialogue-based social manipulation), Nerve Testing (testosterone injection dexterity game)
- **Market Mood System** — Real crypto prices affect NPC behaviour, dialogue, and difficulty. Bull run? Everyone's a genius. Crash? NPCs are depressed and dangerous.
- **AI-Driven Dialogue** — NPC chat lines shift dynamically based on market conditions, region, and player stats
- **Pyramid Progression** — Climb 10 tiers to unlock new regions, abilities, and increasingly absurd hustle opportunities
- **4 Boss Encounters** — Multi-phase confrontations combining trading, dialogue battles, and stat checks
- **Multiple Endings** — Your choices throughout determine the fate of the manosphere
